Egg Foo Young's Recipe

This egg foo young recipe is light, airy, and full of flavor. The thick Chinese sauce, made with Chinese cooking wine and soy sauce, is the perfect finishing touch.

What Is Egg Foo Young?

Egg foo young is a Chinese fried egg omelet drizzled with a savory, soy sauce-based gravy.

usually features

  • sprouts
  • onions
  • some sort of protein (in this case, shrimp).

How to Make Egg Foo Young

  1. Mix the sprouts, shrimp, eggs, green onions, and garlic powder together.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet, form patties, and fry on both sides until golden brown.
  3. Make the sauce on the stove, then simmer until thick.
  4. Spoon the sauce over the patties to serve.
